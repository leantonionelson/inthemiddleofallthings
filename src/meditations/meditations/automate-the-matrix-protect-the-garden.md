Pause here.
List the tasks that drain your presence.

The point is not productivity for its own sake.
It is to protect the unquantifiable:
a walk without destination,
knees in the grass with a child,
a meal that tastes like conversation,
the long look at a tree that resets your breath.

Optimise the scaffolds.
Sanctify the garden.
